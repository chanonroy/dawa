For example:

We made our homemade substitutes to clean and remove soap scums on the shower glass, which is very easy process to make and eco-friendly in feature.

Here is process of making a homemade substitutes to clean a soap scums:

  1. Mix a vinegar and baking soda
  2. This mixture results in paste, which we can apply on the glasses all over using nonscratch scrub
  3. Mix a lemon and vinegar
  4. Spray mixture of lemon and vinegar liquids after 5 to 7 minutes of vinegar and baking soda paste
  5. Give it a complete round of smooth scrubbing
  6. Finally, wipe out with glass wiper and cleaned towel
  7. Cleaned and transparent glass again
