Our Green Story

Dear Friends,
We, Dawa Norbu and Jampa Tsering, are both Tibetan immigrants who have lived in Ottawa for the past few years. Together, we have combined our strengths and experience to build an ambitious community-oriented and 'green' cleaning business called "Himalayan Cleaners".
We believe strongly in using only eco-friendly cleaning equipment to preserve your health as well as the environment. Our professional and knowledgable staff are ready to assist you with whatever cleaning needs you might have.
So give us a call and learn more about the many benefits of eco-friendly cleaning for your home and workplace.
